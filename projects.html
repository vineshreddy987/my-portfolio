Electricity Generation using Piezoelectric Discs

Project Overview

This project demonstrates the conversion of mechanical energy into electrical energy using piezoelectric materials. When pressure is applied to the discs, they generate a voltage that can be harvested and stored.

Key Components

  • Piezoelectric discs (35mm diameter)
  • Full-wave bridge rectifier circuit
  • Capacitors for energy storage (1000μF)
  • Voltage regulator (LM7805)
  • Rechargeable Li-ion battery (3.7V)
  • LED indicators for output monitoring
  • Prototype board for circuit assembly
  • Enclosure with pressure plates

Technologies Used

Piezoelectric Materials Energy Harvesting Circuit Design Power Electronics
